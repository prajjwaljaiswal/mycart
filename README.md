# MyCart - E-Commerce Platform

MyCart is a modern, full-featured e-commerce platform that allows users to shop, create stores, and manage products. It's built with Next.js, React, and PostgreSQL.

### For Shoppers

- **Browse Products**: Explore products from multiple stores
- **Shopping Cart**: Add items to cart and manage quantities
- **Order Management**: Place orders and track their status
- **Product Reviews**: Rate and review products you've purchased
- **Address Management**: Save multiple delivery addresses
- **Coupon System**: Apply discount coupons at checkout

### For Store Owners

- **Create Your Store**: Set up your own online store with a unique username
- **Product Management**: Add, edit, and manage your products
- **Order Tracking**: View and manage orders from your customers
- **Store Dashboard**: Monitor your store's performance

### For Administrators

- **Store Approval**: Review and approve new store registrations
- **Coupon Management**: Create and manage discount coupons
- **Platform Oversight**: Monitor all stores and activities
